Your indicator is now ready for use and will be listed in the Indicator Dialog window.


![CustomSeriesUsing1](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/1hlwceal/production/e5d3fb5c7be8a2b6a77c809b1ad3702556f308f0-660x450.png)


- The indicator can now be found in the "Available" section of the Indicators window
- Once added to the "Configured" section, our user-defined inputs can be entered along with standard indicator properties.


Once applied to a chart, the indicator should look something like the image below.
